Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 4, 2026

Snacks Dictionary is a personal web app for tracking snacks you have tried, including favorites like Pepero, Turtle Chips, and Choco Pie. This page explains what data is used and how.

Information We Use

  • Google account profile data used for sign-in (name, email, profile photo).
  • Snack entries you create: names, brand, ratings, notes, image links, and image file metadata.
  • Image files you upload so they can be stored in your Google Drive.
  • A short-lived Google access token stored in local browser storage for active sessions.

How It Is Used

  • To authenticate you with Google.
  • To save and load your snack dictionary entries.
  • To upload and display your entry images.
  • To keep your session active while using the app.

Where Data Is Stored

Entry data is stored in your Google Drive AppData folder using the drive.appdata scope. Uploaded image files are stored in your Google Drive under Snacks Dictionary/images using the drive.file scope.

Sharing

Your data is not sold. The app only uses the data needed for login and snack entry storage features.

Data Deletion

You can delete your data anytime by removing the AppData file and deleting image files in Snacks Dictionary/images in your Google Drive. Signing out also removes the stored access token from your browser.

Security

The app uses Google sign-in. Passwords are not stored by Snacks Dictionary.

Children's Privacy

Snacks Dictionary is not intended for children under 13.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated. The effective date at the top of this page will be updated when changes are made.
